The documented chokepoint of South Africa's information architecture. Housed at the University of the Witwatersrand, funded by OSF (12%), Luminate (8%), and the Gates Foundation (13%). Chokepoint04

The International Fact-Checking Network: The Guild

The International Fact-Checking Network (IFCN) at the Poynter Institute certifies which organisations Meta, TikTok, and Google use to rate content. Funded by Google, Meta, OSF, and the National Endowment for Democracy (NED). Its president sits on the advisory board that governs it. Complaints about certified fact-checkers are adjudicated by the network itself. No external audit exists.

The standards body was built by the founder of the fact-checker it certifies. The system adjudicates complaints about itself.
