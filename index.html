Between 1979 and 2026, a network of foundations, government agencies, media organisations, and personnel bridges constructed an information architecture across South Africa. The funding is documented. The personnel rotations are on the public record. The editorial outputs are visible.

What has never been assembled in one place is the system itself.

This project maps that system. Every node is sourced from primary documents, court filings, published financial disclosures, leaked internal memos, parliamentary records, or institutional histories. Where evidence is strong, it is stated as fact. Where pattern is visible but direct causation cannot be proven, it is stated as observed pattern.

The investigations are organised into four categories. Networks map institutional relationships. Mechanisms expose operational pipelines. Funding Flows trace documented money trails. Timelines reconstruct chronological sequences no single news article has ever presented.

Start anywhere. The architecture reveals itself.
